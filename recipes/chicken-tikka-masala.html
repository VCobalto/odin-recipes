Chicken Tikka Masala Recipe

Hungry yet? I am.

This chicken tikka masala recipe is an easy and quick version of this famous dish. You can serve it with naan bread or rice. For a vegetarian version of this recipe you can replace chicken with soy curls, tofu, tempeh or your choice of legumes.
Here is all you need to know to prepare this delicious meal.

Ingredients list:

  • 4 skinless chicken breasts
  • 400gr diced tomatoes
  • 60gr plain unsweetened yogurt
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 10gr fresh ginger
  • 1 medium sized onion
  • 2 tablespoons masala curry paste
  • 60ml water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour (or starch)
  • 3 tablespoons fresh cilantro (optional)
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Dice the chicken breasts into 3cm pieces (~1 inch) and set aside.
  2. Coarsly chop the garlic, ginger and tomato.
  3. Combine the tomatoes, yogurt, garlic and ginger in a food processor and process until it becomes a smooth paste.
  4. Coarsly chop the onions, heat the oil in a large frying pan over medium heat. Add the onions to the pan and fry until soft (3-4 minutes), stirring constantly.
  5. Add the curry paste to the onions and fry until fragrant (~1 minute), stirring occasionally.
  6. Add the tomato mixture and chicken to the pan and mix together. You can also add salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Mix the water and flour together in a cup. Remove the pan from the heat and add the mixture to it, mixing well.
  8. Return the pan to the heat and bring to a boil, stirring constantly (~5 minutes).
  9. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pan and cook until thickened (~15 minutes).
  10. If you'd like, chop the cilantro and sprinkle it on top. Serve immediately.
